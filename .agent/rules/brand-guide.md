# Brand Guidelines

## Typography
The brand uses a modern, clean typographic system.
- **Primary Font**: [Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter)
  - Usage: **Headings and Body**. A single typeface ensures a cohesive voice.
- **Secondary Font**: [Fira Code](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Fira+Code)
  - Usage: Code blocks, technical data, and mono-spaced elements.

## Logo & Identity
- **Clear Space**: Maintain a clear space of "X" around the logo, where X is the height of the brand icon.
- **Prohibitions**: Do not alter proportions, change colors outside the palette, or distort the logomark.
- **Brand Name**: Always write "emmanueljet" in lowercase. Never use Title Case (Emmanueljet) or Uppercase (EMMANUELJET).
  - **Titles**: If the brand name appears in a title (e.g., "emmanueljet imager"), the entire title must be lowercase. Never use "emmanueljet Imager".

## Gradient Definitions
- **Primary Gradient**:
  - **Start**: emmanueljet Purple (`#8B15BA`) (Top Left)
  - **End**: emmanueljet Gold-Orange (`#FCA311`) (Bottom Right)
  - **Usage**: Key brand features, conveying dynamism and innovation.

- **Tonal Purple Gradient**:
  - **Stops**: Lavender (`#E6E6FA`) -> Orchid (`#DA70D6`) -> Deep Violet (`#58009A`) -> Purple (`#8B15BA`)
  - **Usage**: Backgrounds requiring depth and tonal variation.

## Implementation Notes
- **Shadows**: Use subtle shadows colored with `#58009A` (Deep Violet) at low opacity.
